# CBIS-DDSM

- **Tier:** A — direct subtlety/perceptibility/conspicuity label
- **Modality / anatomy:** Screen-film mammography (curated, DICOM-converted) / masses and calcifications
- **Finding type:** Breast masses and calcifications

## Subtlety / perceptibility label
Per-lesion **subtlety rating 1–5** (1 = subtle, 5 = obvious). Also: BI-RADS descriptors (mass shape/margin, calcification type/distribution), breast density, BI-RADS assessment 0–5, pathologic outcome.

## Label provenance
Subtlety labels originate from the original DDSM annotators; curated by a trained mammographer.

## Size
~2,620 cases (subset of the original DDSM ~10k cases).

## Access / licensing
Open via TCIA. https://www.cancerimagingarchive.net/collection/cbis-ddsm/

## Caveats
- Subtlety labels originate from the original DDSM annotators; rater count per lesion not always per-reader-resolved.
- Film-digitized — not FFDM.

## Relevance to detectability
Direct subtlety label, large enough for modeling. First-choice feasibility check for porting the detectability framing to mammography.
