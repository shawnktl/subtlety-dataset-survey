# OPTIMAM (OMI-DB)

- **Tier:** A — direct subtlety/perceptibility/conspicuity label
- **Modality / anatomy:** FFDM, DBT (subset), MRI (smaller subset) / breast lesions
- **Finding type:** Breast lesions (masses)

## Subtlety / perceptibility label
Per-lesion ROIs include radiological appearance and **conspicuity** rating, plus BI-RADS. Mass conspicuity is one of the explicit categorization fields.

## Label provenance
Annotated subset (not all images carry conspicuity ratings).

## Size
Very large — 1,311,413 studies from 465,000+ women, ~7M images. DBT for ~4,500 women.

## Access / licensing
Restricted / DUA via Cancer Research Horizons (commercial and academic licensing). https://www.cancerresearchhorizons.com/our-portfolio/our-licensing-opportunities/optimam-mammography-image-database-omi-db

## Caveats
- Not freely downloadable; access requires application + DUA.
- Conspicuity coverage is on the annotated subset, not all images.
- **Verify before relying on:** the DBT subset (~4,500 women) is much smaller than the FFDM core. Whether conspicuity annotations cover the DBT subset uniformly is not stated in public materials.

## Relevance to detectability
Direct conspicuity label at substantial scale. Natural scale-up after CBIS-DDSM for mammography — but access friction is real.
